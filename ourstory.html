 Introduction 

Los Angeles is a land of opportunity, glory, dead ends, and inequality. Our backyard is a city where the poor are born into their poverty, and remain in their poverty. Resolve Los Angeles was formed knowing that this issue may not be solved with charity alone, but with hope that it could make a difference for as many as it can reach.

 William Simpson 
  Founder, Chief Financial Officer, UI Developer 

Originally born in Connecticut, but having moved Los Angeles at a young age, and remaining in the city since. Driven to make a meaningful impact for Los Angeles disadvantaged, William made use of his passion for business and computer science to form Resolve Los Angeles’ with the help of his friends and schoolmates.

 Franklin Harrow 
 Head Art Director, Publicity 

Born and raised in Los Angeles, and working towards attending University in pursuit of eventually becoming a Fashion Designer, Franklin was approached by William Simpson about helping to create the designs for Resolve Los Angeles’ first collections, and now works as its Head of Design.

 Fares Abdelrazig 
  Social Media Manager 

Another native to Los Angeles and an aspiring medical worker, Fares was asked by William to assist with marketing and drawing attention to the mission of Resolve Los Angeles and now works as its Head of Marketing.

 Vivienne Moncure 
 Outreach, Publicity 

Looking forward to traveleling and experiencing different cultures, and with a passion for helping others, Vivienne Moncure found great interest in helping Los Angeles communities. So when William Simpson came to her with Resolve Los Angeles she was exited to work with him, later bec the head of outreach and publicity.
